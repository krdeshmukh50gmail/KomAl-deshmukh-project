# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end **sales analytics dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using an online retail dataset. The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow including data cleaning, transformation, dashboard creation, and business insight generation.

The objective was to convert raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that enables users to explore sales performance, customer activity, geographic distribution, and purchasing trends.

---

# Business Objective

The primary goals of this project are to:

- Clean and prepare raw retail transaction data.
- Analyze sales performance across customers, countries, invoices, and products.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business reporting.
- Identify important sales trends and revenue drivers.
- Generate actionable business insights from the analysis.

---

# Dataset Description

The workbook contains historical online retail transaction data including:

- Invoice information
- Customer purchases
- Product descriptions
- Sales quantities
- Revenue
- Country-wise transactions
- Invoice summaries

The analysis focuses on understanding overall business performance and identifying trends that support better business decisions.

# Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Distribution

- Domestic customers contribute approximately **90%** of total revenue.
- International sales account for approximately **10%**, indicating opportunities for market expansion.

### 2. Seasonal Sales Trend

- Sales increase significantly from **September through November**.
- These months represent the strongest sales period in the dataset.

> **Note:** December 2011 data may be incomplete. Any decline observed during December should be interpreted with caution and should not be assumed to represent an actual reduction in business performance.

### 3. Product Performance

The highest revenue-generating products include:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT
- LITTLE BIRDIE

The top three products together contribute approximately **47% of total sales**, highlighting a concentration of revenue among a small number of products.

### 4. Geographic Reach

- Sales were recorded across **38 countries**.
- The business has a broad international customer base despite most revenue coming from domestic sales.

### 5. Invoice Performance

- Total invoices processed: **19,960**
- Average invoice value: **$533.171**

These metrics provide a high-level view of transaction volume and average customer purchase value.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Increase inventory planning ahead of the September–November peak sales season.
- Continue monitoring high-performing products to maintain availability during periods of high demand.
- Explore opportunities to increase international sales, which currently represent a relatively small share of total revenue.
- Use customer and invoice analysis to identify opportunities for improving repeat purchases and average order value.
- Continue tracking sales trends over complete reporting periods before drawing conclusions from partial monthly data.
